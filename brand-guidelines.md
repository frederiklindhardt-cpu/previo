## Typography

- **Display / Headings:** Instrument Serif (regular weight, no italic on accent words)
- **Body / UI:** DM Sans (weights: 400 body, 500 labels/buttons, 600 strong emphasis — sparingly)
- **Body line-height:** 1.7 on light backgrounds, 1.6 on dark
- **Heading emphasis:** Use teal-coloured `<span>` — never italic `<em>` on coloured words

---

## Logo

- **Text mark:** "Previo." in Instrument Serif, period is teal `#0D9488`
- **On dark:** Text in `#F1F3F7`, dot in `#0D9488`
- **On light:** Text in `#111827`, dot in `#0D9488`
- **Minimum size:** 14px
- **SVG P mark:** Favicon and app icon only. Do not use on the website or in emails.

## Voice & Tone

**Write as if the reader is a compliance officer who has seen 50 vendor pitches this year.**

### Use
- "automated" (not "AI-powered")
- "structured" (not "revolutionary")
- "auditable" (not "game-changing")
- "defensible" (not "cutting-edge")

### Avoid
- "Excited to announce..."
- "Game-changing", "revolutionary", "cutting-edge"
- "AI-powered" (even if AI is used internally)
- Exclamation marks in professional copy
- Emoji in headings or opening lines

### Tone
- Corporate, measured, specific
- Quiet confidence — let the product speak
- Direct sentences, short paragraphs
- Lead with the problem, not the solution

---

## Target Market Language

Previo targets **all regulated and reputation-sensitive hiring** — not just financial services.

**Always list in this order (rotate, don't use all every time):**
1. Regulated firms (financial services, legal, healthcare, insurance)
2. Political parties and public bodies
3. Professional services (consultancies, advisory, accountancies)
4. Senior leadership and board appointments

**Never** lead with "financial services" alone. The common thread is reputational and regulatory vulnerability.

## Spacing

- Generous whitespace everywhere. When in doubt, add more padding.
- Section padding: 7-9rem vertically on desktop, 4-5rem on mobile
- Between elements: favour 1.5-2rem gaps over tight spacing

## LinkedIn Post Style Guide

### Text Formatting
- Opening line: direct, no emoji. Start with a statement or question.
- Body: short paragraphs (1-2 sentences). Line breaks between paragraphs.
- No hashtags in the body. 2-3 at the very end, after a blank line.
- Hashtag rotation: `#PreEmploymentScreening` `#RegulatoryCompliance` `#DueDiligence` `#HiringRisk` `#BackgroundScreening`

### Tone
- Measured, authoritative, slightly dry
- The voice of a senior compliance advisor, not a startup founder
- No exclamation marks. No "We're excited to announce..."

### Graphics (if used)
- Light background (`#FAFBFC`), teal accent, DM Sans for text
- Card-style: white card, subtle border, teal highlight bar on left edge, dark text
- No dark-mode graphics (they read as AI-generated on LinkedIn)
- No gradient backgrounds
- Previo wordmark ("Previo." with teal dot) in bottom-right corner

### Content Pillars
1. **Industry insight** — screening failures in the news (analysis, not gloating)
2. **Process transparency** — how good screening works, what to look for
3. **Market education** — why political parties / professional services need screening
4. **Product signals** — waitlist updates, feature previews (max 1x/month)
