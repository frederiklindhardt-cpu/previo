1. Go to **Loops** (left sidebar) in app.loops.so → **Create Loop**
2. Set trigger: **"Contact added to mailing list"** → select **Waitlist** (`cmnqhdkx72enl0i1o3w7922ck`)
3. Add 5 email steps with the delays below
4. Paste the subject line and body for each email
5. Set the sender to `hello@previo-group.com` (or whichever address you want replies going to)

The sequence auto-fires for every new waitlist signup. Existing contacts already on the list won't be retroactively enrolled — only new additions trigger the flow.

## Email 3 — Day 7

**Subject:** How Previo actually works (in 60 seconds)

**Body:**

Here's the process, start to finish:

**1. You submit a candidate.** Name, email, and the role they're being considered for. That's it.

**2. They consent.** We send a secure invite. The candidate provides consent, personal details, and employment history — fully GDPR compliant.

**3. Automated checks run.** Sanctions and watchlists, adverse media, employment verification against Companies House and regulatory registers, and professional register lookups (FCA, PRA, SRA, and others).

**4. You get a clear answer.** Every candidate receives a score from 0 to 100:
- **80–100 (Clear):** No material findings. Report delivered automatically.
- **50–79 (Caution):** Findings that warrant attention. Human-reviewed before it reaches you.
- **0–49 (Do Not Proceed):** Serious flags. Human-reviewed, delivered with full context.

One score. One report. Audit trail attached. Hours, not weeks.

We're finalising the platform now. Waitlist members get first access.

— The Previo Team
